# Photos

Public-safe photos may be added later as research evidence.

## Allowed Later

- A clean photo of the Unitree GO2 Air hardware.
- A photo suitable for a recruiter or research portfolio.
- A screenshot or thumbnail from a future safe demo.

## Do Not Include

- Serial numbers
- Private location details
- Private network information
- Credentials or screens containing tokens
- Unsafe operation
- Photos implying autonomous behavior that has not been demonstrated

## Claim Boundary

Photos are visual evidence only. They should not imply completed ROS2 integration, autonomous navigation, production deployment, or a shipped robotics product.
